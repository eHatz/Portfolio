About Me

I have always been interested in technology and how it brings people together. As Full Stack Web Developer I have made it my goal to create simple and clean user interfaces which allow users to easily access the information that they seek. I love designing web applications and games which allow users to connect and spend time with friends and family. I am a quick study, and enjoy working on challenging projects which allow me to break down and understand problems in order to find a solution.
